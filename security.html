Security

✓ No known open vulnerabilities

Verisyn handles real accounts and real payments, so we take security seriously. Here's what's actually in place, and how to report a problem if you find one.

What we do

  • Passwords are salted and hashed (scrypt) — we never store or see your actual password
  • New passwords are checked against known breach databases at signup
  • Session cookies are HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite-protected
  • Login and account-creation attempts are rate limited to prevent brute-forcing
  • All payments are processed by Paystack — we never see or store your card details
  • Every paid feature is enforced server-side, not just hidden in the interface
  • Payment webhooks are cryptographically signature-verified
  • Standard security headers and a Content-Security-Policy are set on every page

What we don't claim

We're a small, independently-built product. We have not undergone formal penetration testing, and we don't hold SOC2 or similar enterprise certifications. We'd rather tell you that plainly than imply a level of assurance we haven't earned yet.

Found a vulnerability?

If you find a genuine security issue, please report it privately rather than posting it publicly — we'll fix real issues quickly and acknowledge your help.

Report a vulnerability
Email us with details and steps to reproduce. We can't currently offer a paid bounty, but we'll credit you publicly (with your permission) once it's fixed.
