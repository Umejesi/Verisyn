Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Verisyn ("we", "us") provides a token and wallet safety scanner via our website, Telegram bot, and browser extension. This page explains what data we collect, why, and how it's handled.

What we collect

  • Account info: if you create an account, we store your email address and a securely hashed password (we never store your actual password). If you sign in with Google, we store the email address Google provides.
  • Scan activity: contract addresses you scan, and — if you use the watchlist feature — the addresses you save, so we can check them and alert you to changes.
  • Payment info: we do not process or store your card details. Payments are handled entirely by Paystack; we only receive confirmation that a payment succeeded and the plan you subscribed to.
  • Technical data: your IP address is used briefly to enforce fair-use scan limits and prevent abuse. We do not sell or share this data.
  • Cookies: a single session cookie to keep you signed in. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Third-party services we use

Verisyn relies on the following services to function. Data relevant to your request (e.g. a contract address you scan) is sent to these providers as part of normal operation:

  • GoPlus Security & DexScreener — contract and market data (no personal data sent)
  • Google — Sign-In authentication, and Gemini AI for generating plain-English scan explanations
  • Etherscan — blockchain transaction history for Pro+ features
  • Paystack — payment processing
  • Resend — transactional emails (sign-in links, watchlist alerts)
  • Upstash — our database, hosting account and scan-related data

How we use your data

  • To provide the scanning, watchlist, and alert features you use
  • To enforce free-tier limits fairly
  • To send you emails you've requested (sign-in links, watchlist alerts, support replies)
  • To prevent abuse (rate limiting, fraud prevention)

We do not sell your data to advertisers or third parties.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account data at any time by emailing us (below). We'll act on deletion requests within a reasonable time.

Children's privacy

Verisyn is not directed at, and should not be used by, anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes meaningfully, we'll update the date at the top of this page.

Questions or requests?
Email us and we'll get back to you.
