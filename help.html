Getting Started
How do I scan a token? ▾
Paste a contract address (or search by token name) into the box on the homepage, pick the chain if it's not auto-detected, and hit Scan. You'll get a risk score, plain-English flags, and market data in a few seconds — no wallet connection needed.
Why is my risk score low even though the token looks fine? ▾
The score reflects real on-chain signals: mint/ownership permissions, tax, liquidity lock, and holder concentration. A low score means at least one of those genuinely triggered — expand "Why this matters" on each flag to see exactly why it counts against the score. It's not a guess; it's specific.
Do I need to connect my wallet? ▾
No — never. Verisyn only reads public blockchain data. You never connect a wallet or sign anything to use any part of the product.
Subscriptions & Billing
How do subscriptions work? ▾
Pro and Pro+ are real recurring subscriptions billed monthly through Paystack (in NGN, shown in USD for clarity). Payment activates your account automatically — no manual steps.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes. Go to your Account page and click Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the billing period you already paid for — no immediate cutoff, no penalty.
What's the difference between Pro and Pro+? ▾
Pro covers unlimited scans, wallet reputation checks, and unlimited watchlists with real email alerts. Pro+ adds AI Q&A on scans, deployer reputation history, rug timelines, early-buyer detection, and PDF/CSV export.
Account & Security
How do I verify my account? ▾
There's no separate identity verification step — signing in (via Google, a magic email link, or email+password) is all you need.
I forgot my password — now what? ▾
Use "Continue with Email Link" on the sign-in page instead of a password — it logs you straight in via a secure emailed link, no reset flow needed.
Features
What is whale analysis? ▾
It shows exactly who holds a token — the top 10 wallets, what % they control, and (Pro+) the deployer's track record across every other token they've made, plus who bought earliest.
What's a honeypot? ▾
A contract designed to let you buy but block you from ever selling — one of the most common total-loss scams. Verisyn checks for this on every scan automatically.
How do I install the Chrome extension? ▾
It's not on the Chrome Web Store yet. For now: download the extension zip, unzip it, go to chrome://extensions, turn on Developer Mode, and click "Load unpacked" to select the folder.
How does the Telegram bot work? ▾
Message @Verisyn_bot and type /scan followed by a contract address in any chat. Group admins can also turn on auto-scan so it checks every address posted automatically.
